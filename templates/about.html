About Dispatch

What is Dispatch?

Dispatch is a Tor-only, privacy-focused file-sharing platform where users can send encrypted files to each other with complete anonymity. Files are temporary, encrypted client-side, and even the platform owner cannot access user content.

How It Works

  • Files are encrypted in your browser before upload
  • Recipients must accept files before they become accessible
  • Files auto-delete after 7 days
  • No IP addresses are ever logged

Privacy Features

  • Tor-only access
  • Client-side encryption
  • End-to-end encrypted chat
  • No tracking or analytics
  • Automatic file expiration
