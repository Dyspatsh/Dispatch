Why I Built Dispatch

I created Dispatch because I believe privacy is not a luxury—it's a right. While big tech companies profit from your data, I built a platform where your files and messages belong to you. No tracking. No logs. No compromises.

Dispatch is hosted on my own server, running entirely on the Tor network. Every file is encrypted before it leaves your browser, and every message is secured with end-to-end encryption. I built this because I wanted a truly private way to share files and communicate, and I'm sharing it with you.

My Mission

To provide a secure, anonymous, and truly private platform for file sharing and communication—free from surveillance, tracking, and data collection. Privacy should be the default, not an afterthought.
