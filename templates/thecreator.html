Why I Built Dispatch

I created Dispatch because I wanted a private way to share files on the Tor network. Existing solutions either had ads, tracking, or were not designed for Tor. I built this platform to address that gap.

Dispatch runs on my own server as a Tor hidden service. Files are encrypted before upload. Messages auto-delete after 24 hours. The platform has no advertisements of any kind.

No ads. No popups. No banners. No sponsored content.

My Mission

To provide a usable, ad-free file sharing and communication platform for the Tor network. Privacy tools should be accessible without tolerating advertisements or tracking.
